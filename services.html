BATHROOMS

Not all bathrooms are created equal.

Sure, they all serve the same basic function, but some are private, peaceful retreats while others are high-traffic areas where kids and even pets enjoy (or resist!) tub time. Whatever the needs of your household, we’ll design a bathroom that enhances both your home and your daily routine. 

  • Sound investment: Increase your home's appeal, reduce water use, and prevent costly failures and repairs down the line.
  • Conscientious craftsmanship: We use only high-quality, warrantied products and materials, assisting you with strategic selections to stretch your dollar and strike a balance between low-maintenance durability and high-impact appearance.
  • More in store: Gain more space, more privacy, and more light. Maximize comfort and convenience.
  • Custom solutions: In addition to tilework, plumbing, lighting design, and other specialized skills, we offer custom features including shower benches and niches, smart storage solutions, and furniture-style vanities.
KITCHENS

Revitalize the heart of your home.

The kitchen is the hub of home life and a gathering place for houseguests. It should reflect who you are and how you live. We’ve got these goals covered as well as your functionality, efficiency, and style requirements, whether you opt for a kitchen facelift with a few upgrades or a full remodel with a new footprint.

  • Return on investment: Kitchen remodeling yields the highest ROI and holds the most appeal for homebuyers, according to the National Association of Realtors.
  • Space creators: We can reconfigure cabinets and countertops to give you more workspace and storage, or remove walls to create an open floor plan.
  • Efficiency and enjoyment: We’ll design a workflow that facilitates your food preparation style and the various tasks and interactions that take place in your kitchen.
  • Hand-holding as needed: A kitchen remodel involves many decisions, from appliance options on down to cabinet knobs. We guide you through the process with suggestions based on years of experience and familiarity with various manufacturers and materials.
 
CARPENTRY

Bringing vision and precision to the craft.

Carpentry is the cornerstone of our company. A second-generation carpenter, Nate Rosalie offers an impressive range of skills to meet your needs, from crafting your home’s “bones” (framing) to its embellishments (ornamental woodwork). His artistry finds its fullest expression in custom cabinetry and millwork, which he designs, builds, and finishes. 

  • Expert installations: We frame additions, hang cabinets, install windows and doors, construct columns and beams, and lay wood flooring.
  • Interior woodwork: We fabricate and install cabinetry, fireplace mantels, closet organization systems, and built-ins including bookcases, shelving, media centers, banquet seating, and mudroom cubbies.
  • Exterior woodwork: We design and install decks, porches, and pergolas.
  • Finishing touches: Dress up your home with molding and trim work, wainscoting, beadboard, or ceiling coffers. 
 
BUILT-INS

Custom pieces add function and flair.

Need floor-to-ceiling shoe cubbies? A coffee bar in the master bedroom? A cabinet to house 55-pound bags of dog food? There’s a solution for that. Custom built-ins add utility, make the most of available space, and provide solutions for specific needs.

  • Stow or show: Custom-designed for each space, built-ins maximize every square inch and add valuable storage and display space. 
  • Blends beautifully: Our custom built-ins complement your existing architecture and woodwork, so they look like part of the original home design.  
  • Specialized spaces: We can design a craft station, wine storage, a cozy reading nook, or a veritable library—whatever accommodates your passions and possessions.
  • Less mess: Built-ins contain clutter—coats and backpacks in the entryway, appliances on countertops, shoe heaps in the closet, piles of paperwork, tons of toiletries. 
 
DESIGN SERVICES

We create a shared vision with you from the start. 

Our design services mark the start of every remodeling project. Listening to your ideas and desires, we create preliminary concepts and develop them into detailed drawings of your ideal design based on your feedback. By documenting all design decisions and conducting a site visit before work begins, we lock in accurate pricing. 

  • Define your vision: During the design phase, we bring your vision into sharp focus by clarifying your needs and preferences.
  • Drawings and details: We create floor plans and 3-D renderings for your project and help you fill in all the details including product, material, and finish selections. 
  • Fixed price: The price we give following the design phase and site visit is not a quote or an estimate. It is a promise.    
 
LIVING SPACES

We can refresh or revamp any space you choose.

Look around your home. If there’s an indoor or outdoor living space that’s tired, dated, dark, cramped, dysfunctional or simply lacking, you don’t have to live with it any longer. Listening to your frustrations and desires, we will address issues, fulfill wishes, and breathe new life into the space.  

  • Reinvent and repurpose: Certain rooms fail to meet the needs of modern and evolving families. We’ll transform unused formal sitting and dining rooms, outgrown bedrooms, isolated kitchens, and master suites that provide neither practicalities nor pampering experiences. 
  • According to need: Often, changing circumstances or priorities are the reason for a remodel. We’ll add space for your growing family, create a main-floor bed and bath if stairs become an issue, or build a home office.
  • Basement finishing: Transform your basement into a bright, beckoning space for your family and guests. 
  • Back to nature: Increase your usable living space with a patio enclosure, sunroom, or outdoor dining area.  
 
AGING IN PLACE

Make life easier now and down the line.

Aging in place is a design approach that enables people to remain in their homes safely, independently, and comfortably regardless of age or ability. We specialize in finding home modification solutions that are attractive as well as functional so you can remain where your fondest memories reside.  

  • Creature comforts: Some modifications not only make life easier later on but also in the here and now, including a bench seat in the shower and a taller “comfort height” toilet.
  • Mobility modifications: From wider doorways to curbless showers, we can equip your home for wheelchair and walker accessibility as well as diminished dexterity and eyesight.
  • The gift of grab: You might not need grab bars now, but for bathroom remodels we recommend reinforcing the walls so they can be easily installed later. You don’t have to settle for institutional grab bars; like a high-end hotel, we can disguise them as artistic design elements.
