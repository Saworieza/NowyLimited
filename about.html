ABOUT US

ACTUALLY, WE'RE ALL ABOUT YOU.

Nowy limited is a privately held consulting-oriented limited company founded in 2018 and headquartered in Kenya. We have branches in South Africa and DR Congo and a staff of highly qualified and multi-skilled engineers and researchers in various areas such as telecommunications, information technology and automation.

The group provides Software solutions, engineering Solutions, Professional services, Managed Services, Telecom Assurance services, installation and Expertise to organizations as well as to individuals.

We are a great ICT solutions provider and serve our clients as trusted and loyal allies providing them the best solutions and services to help their respective businesses flourish.

Nowy limited analyses African needs and niches in order to fill the gap by providing appropriate solutions, products or services that will satisfy the customers businesses.

 

Our Approach

Nowy Limited is a customer centric company that focuses on providing the right solutions and services to our targeted customers at affordable prices with flexible payments.

We approach every project as a partnership with the customer during which we explore all the possibilities to offer the best technical solutions that will satisfy the customer needs at affordable cost.
